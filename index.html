About

Hey there! I'm Karim Haddad, a Technical Artist based in Cairo, Egypt. I've been into gaming since way back, starting with modding online games. Now, I'm a Freelance Technical Artist, working on cool projects like XR apps and simulations.

If you need a Technical Artist with a passion for gaming and a knack for making things look cool, let's chat! Feel free to reach out, and let's create something amazing together.
